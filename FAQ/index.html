  • How Do I

  • Chooseing sides in a conflict zone?

    In case you are wondering how to pick a side in a conflict zone, the correct way of doing it is by entering the conflict zone, then accessing the FUNCTIONS tab on the right HUD menu (Yes, the same tab where you can manually turn on lights, retract cargo scoop etc...) The very first option (FACTION) should now allow you to pick a side. Once you do this, all ships will turn red and green accordingly and you will automatically collect bounties on red kills.

  • Tips and Tricks

  • Planetary landings- Dismiss your ship after leaving it with your SRV!

    As long as there's no reason to have it sitting on the surface ALWAYS send it into orbit. You can recall it any time and you don't have to be afraid that it gets shot to pieces on the surface.

  • Approaching Coriolis Stations

    If you want to drop out of supercruise at the entrance side of a Station,approach from the inside of it's orbit since the entrance is mostly pointed towards the body it´s orbiting.

  • Far out Exploration

    Get yourself AFMs (Auto field maintenance units) Scooping stars - and especially using neutron star boosts will damage your ship bit by bit. So if you plan to leave the bubble for a while, get as many as you can and also mind that you can reload them with materials you find on bodies. That´s how you survive out there for as long as you need if you watch it!

  • Engineering - up your modules

    If you don't watch it, you might end up with modules that consume more energy than your ship can support. Always have a look at the capabilities of your ship in delivering power and better upgrade those first before you crank up the damage or shield power of your ship!

  • Why do i suddenly get attacked frequently by pirates and fined / attacked by system authority?

    If you recently picked up cargo from shot down ships, chances are good that you picked up "illicit cargo". Mind that certain factions have different fews on what is "illicit" though

  • How do i acquire system permits?

    There are Two ways to gain a system permit. You Get some specific system permits Via Ranking up with the Empire, Federation and Alliance. To Get a System permit that the Major powers don't give. You will have to make friends with the controlling faction near said system. System Permit List is Here

  • Why do i struggle making Money / gaining Ranks / progressing overall ?

    Mostly because you tried to rush things. Approach Elite with the pace of a regular MMO and not of a space shooter. Build up ties with systems you think are profitable for you by running missions. That will provide better paid missions in the future. Grind your way through to engineer - up your modules to be more capable and efficient. Even though the are random loopholes from time to time that might get you a progress boost, chances are good that if you get cocky space will put you back into your place.
